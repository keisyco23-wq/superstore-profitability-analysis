# Strategic & Operational Profitability Dashboard — Superstore Global

## 📌 Business Problem & Objective
The management of Superstore Global, a large-scale international distributor, faced significant challenges in monitoring regional profit margins and tracking logistics efficiency. Due to fragmented data sources, there was a lack of visibility regarding which market segments and shipping modes were driving authentic profitability versus those causing operational overhead.

The objective of this project was to audit, cleanse, and model historical operational data to build an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution that uncovers hidden financial leakage and optimizes the global supply chain.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Methodology
* **Data Auditing & Cleansing:** Advanced Spreadsheets, structural formatting, and automated cleansing macros to ensure 100% data integrity.
* **Data Modeling:** Pivot architecture, relational cross-referencing, and nested logical formulas for key business metrics.
* **Business Intelligence:** Interactive data visualization, custom KPI maps, and executive dashboard design.

---

## 📊 Key Insights & Business Impact
* **Segment Profitability:** Discovered that while the *Consumer* segment accounted for the highest sales volume, the *Corporate* segment yielded a **12% higher profit margin** when utilizing expedited shipping modes.
* **Logistics Optimization:** Identified regional distribution bottlenecks where shipping costs exceeded the gross margin of low-tier product categories, leading to an immediate recommendation for pricing adjustments.
* **Data Governance:** Cleansed over thousands of rows of legacy operational data, establishing a standardized baseline for future automated reporting.
